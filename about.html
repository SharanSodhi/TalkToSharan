💼 About Sharanjeet

A Nutrition and Dietetics expert with over 8 years of diverse experience in clinical, bariatric, and community nutrition, Sharanjeet blends science, empathy, and innovation in everything she does. From leading hospital dietetics teams in India to providing personalized support in Canada, her portfolio spans therapeutic care, education, public speaking, and outreach.

With a strong foundation in therapeutic nutrition and a passion for holistic health, she continues to make a meaningful impact in both clinical settings and public health awareness.
